What is Startup India Registration?

Startup India registration is an online process under the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) for recognising innovative businesses. This recognition supports growth, provides access to startup-specific benefits and improves credibility for networking and investments.

With an approved Startup India recognition, businesses get stronger market visibility and better acceptance from customers, corporates and government bodies.

Who is this service for?

  • Early-stage startups developing innovative solutions.
  • New companies or LLPs less than 10 years old.
  • Founders planning funding or accelerator programs.
  • Businesses needing government recognition for credibility.

Key Benefits

  • Official Startup Recognition Certificate.
  • Better acceptance for funding and partnerships.
  • Priority in govt. programs and innovation support.
  • Higher trust with customers and corporates.
  • Improved brand credibility.

Documents Required (Basic)

  • Founders' Aadhaar + PAN.
  • Incorporation certificate of Company/LLP.
  • Brief pitch / business write-up.
  • Website or product details (if any).
  • Business activities and objectives.

Quick FAQs

Is Startup India recognition mandatory for all startups?

No, but it helps significantly in credibility, programs, and opportunities.

Will I get tax exemption automatically?

No. Tax exemption (80-IAC) is a separate approval based on eligibility.

Can a sole proprietorship apply?

No, Startup India recognition is for Company, LLP or Registered Partnership.
