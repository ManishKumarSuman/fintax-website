What is ITR Filing?

Income Tax Return filing is the process of reporting your yearly income, tax deductions, investments and tax liability to the Income Tax Department. Filing correctly helps you stay compliant and receive refunds on time.

With professional filing, you avoid common mistakes, mismatches and unnecessary notices from the department.

Who is this service for?

  • Salaried individuals
  • Freelancers and professionals
  • Small business owners
  • People having rental income or capital gains
  • Anyone expecting tax refund

Documents Required

  • PAN, Aadhaar
  • Form 16 (if salaried)
  • Bank statements
  • Details of investments and deductions
  • Rental income or capital gain details
  • Business income details, if applicable

Quick FAQs

Is ITR filing compulsory for everyone?

If your income crosses the taxable limit or you want to claim refunds, then filing is mandatory.

How long do refunds take?

Usually a few weeks after successful return processing.

Can you file ITR for past years?

Yes, revised and belated returns can be filed depending on current rules.
