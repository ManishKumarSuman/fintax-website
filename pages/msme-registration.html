What is MSME (Udyam) Registration?

MSME (Udyam) registration is an online process that recognises micro, small and medium enterprises under government rules. It provides official classification based on investment and turnover criteria and opens doors to various support schemes and concessions.

Who is this service for?

  • Small manufacturers and workshop owners.
  • Traders, suppliers and retail businesses.
  • Service providers, consultants and professionals running small concerns.
  • Startups and micro-enterprises seeking formal recognition.

Key Benefits

  • Eligibility for government subsidies and schemes.
  • Easier access to credit and priority lending from banks.
  • Preference in government procurement and tenders.
  • Reduced interest rates under some schemes.
  • Formal business identity and better market credibility.

Documents Required (Basic)

  • PAN of proprietor / entity.
  • Aadhar of proprietor / authorised person.
  • Proof of business address (rent agreement, electricity bill).
  • Bank account details and cancelled cheque.
  • Basic product/service description and investment/turnover details.

Quick FAQs

Is MSME registration compulsory?

It is not compulsory, but highly recommended to avail benefits and formal recognition.

Can I update details after registration?

Yes, Udyam allows updating certain details as the business grows.

Does MSME registration require auditors?

For small businesses, basic documents are enough; auditors are required only in specific situations.
