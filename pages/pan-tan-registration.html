What is PAN & TAN Registration?

PAN is a unique 10-character identifier issued by the Income Tax Department for tax and financial identity. TAN is a 10-character number required for entities deducting or collecting tax at source.

Both are required for proper tax reporting and compliance under Indian laws.

Who is this service for?

  • Individuals applying for PAN for the first time.
  • Businesses / entities requiring TAN for TDS compliance.
  • Companies, firms and employers who need TAN for deducting TDS.
  • People with PAN mismatch or needing corrections.

Key Benefits

  • Legal identity for tax and financial transaction.
  • Required for ITR filing, bank operations and investments.
  • Ensures correct TDS credits for vendors/recipients.
  • Minimizes future compliance notices and errors.

Documents Required (Basic)

  • For PAN (individual): Aadhaar, proof of identity/address, passport-size photo (depending on mode).
  • For TAN (entity): PAN of entity (if applicable), proof of business, address proof and authorised signatory details.

Quick FAQs

How long does PAN allotment take?

PAN is usually allotted within a few working days after successful submission and verification.

Can PAN details be corrected later?

Yes, corrections (PAN change/updates) can be filed if necessary.

Who needs TAN?

Any person/entity responsible for deducting TDS must obtain TAN.
