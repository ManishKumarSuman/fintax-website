What is IEC Registration?

IEC (Import Export Code) is a key business identification number required by businesses and individuals engaging in import/export activities in India. It is issued by DGFT (Directorate General of Foreign Trade) and is mandatory for customs clearance and shipping.

Who is this service for?

  • Traders and manufacturers planning to export goods.
  • Importers bringing products or raw materials into India.
  • Service exporters (IT, consultancy) providing services overseas.
  • Export houses and e-commerce sellers shipping abroad.

Key Benefits

  • Legal permission to carry out international trade.
  • Eligibility for export incentives and schemes.
  • Smooth customs clearance and shipping processes.
  • Easier banking and foreign exchange transactions.

Documents Required (Basic)

  • PAN of business or proprietor.
  • Proof of business address (rent agreement, utility bill).
  • Bank account details and cancelled cheque.
  • GST registration (if applicable).
  • IEC application with authorised signatory details.

Quick FAQs

Is IEC required for small exporters?

Yes, any person/entity exporting or importing goods/services needs an IEC.

How long does IEC registration take?

Usually a few working days if documents are complete and correct.

Can IEC be used for multiple branches?

IEC is issued to the entity and can be used for export/import activities by that entity.
