Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to file an Income Tax Return (ITR)?

In most normal cases, once we receive all documents, ITR filing can be completed within 1–2 working days.

What basic documents are usually required?

Common documents include PAN, Aadhaar, bank statement, salary slip or income details, and last year’s return if available. For registrations and licenses, additional documents may be needed.

Do I need to visit your office for every service?

No. Many services can be completed through phone, WhatsApp and email. Physical visit is required only when necessary.

Are your charges fixed and transparent?

Yes. We inform you about the professional charges and government fees in advance. There are no hidden costs.

Can you help if my previous filings have issues?

Yes, we can review your previous returns or registrations and guide you on corrections, rectifications or new filings as required.
